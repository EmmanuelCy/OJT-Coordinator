Description: 
The On the Job (OJT) Coordinator Management System is a web-based software that manages trainee profiles and training programs. 
It allows users to create and manage trainee profiles, create and manage training programs, track trainee performance, 
manage training resources, and generate reports and analytics. The system is scalable and user-friendly, 
suitable for organizations of all sizes.
